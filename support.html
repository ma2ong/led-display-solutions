Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the lifespan of your LED displays?

Our LED displays are engineered for a lifespan of over 100,000 hours of operation under normal conditions. This is supported by our use of high-quality LEDs, efficient power supplies, and excellent thermal management.

What kind of warranty do you offer?

We offer a standard 2-year warranty on all our products, with options to extend up to 5 years. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and component failures. Please contact our sales team for detailed warranty information.

Do you provide installation support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support including remote installation guidance, on-site supervision, and full turnkey installation services depending on the project scope and your requirements.
