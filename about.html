About Us

Pioneering LED Display Technology Since 2007

Our Story

Founded in 2007, Shenzhen Lianjin Optoelectronics Co., Ltd. has grown into a globally recognized high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, development, manufacturing, and sales of LED displays. With a registered capital of 20.1 million RMB and a state-of-the-art 50,000 m² factory, we are dedicated to delivering excellence and innovation in every pixel.

Our Mission

To provide professional, reliable, and cutting-edge LED display solutions that empower our clients to visualize their ideas and connect with their audiences effectively. We are committed to sustainable development, technological advancement, and unparalleled customer service.

Corporate Culture

Our culture is built on a foundation of integrity, innovation, and customer-centricity. We foster a collaborative environment where every team member is empowered to contribute to our collective success and push the boundaries of what's possible in LED technology.

Enterprise Honors

Our commitment to quality and innovation has been recognized by numerous certifications and awards.
